WallFlex™
Biliary RX Stent

"We always knew the WallFlex stent was capable of helping more patients, but the efforts of a cross-functional team turned that potential into a reality."
- Mark Wood, R&D Engineer

Performing at the highest level to help more patients

Collaborating to find new ways to treat more patients is what we do at Boston Scientific. A good example is the WallFlexTM Biliary RX Stent, a leading stent designed for the palliative treatment of malignant biliary strictures, commonly caused by pancreatic cancer. A team of employees representing Clinical, R&D, Regulatory and Marketing focused on expanding the WallFlex indications to include treatment of benign biliary strictures. Together, they evaluated physician feedback, tested the stent and utilized clinical data to obtain an expanded European indication for WallFlex to be used in the treatment of both benign biliary strictures and those produced by malignant tumors. The result: successful treatment of more patients with the potential to improve the patients’ experience, to reduce the number of procedures they require and to decrease the overall cost of care.
